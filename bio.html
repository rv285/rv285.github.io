I am Professor of Information Engineering at the University of Cambridge, and a Staff Fellow at Trinity Hall. I am part of the Probabilistic Systems, Information, and Inference (PSI2) Group in the Division of Information Engineering.

I received my PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2008, and my undergraduate degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras in 2002. Before joining the University of Cambridge in 2013, I held post-doctoral positions at Stanford University and Yale University.

My research interests are broadly in information theory, high-dimensional statistics, and machine learning. I am currently an Area Editor for the IEEE Transactions on Information Theory, and have previously served as Associate Editor for the IEEE Transactions on Information Theory and the IEEE Transactions on Communications.
